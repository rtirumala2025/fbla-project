# Presentation Playbook

Use this script to guide the live demonstration and to prepare the contingency recording.

## Live Demo Flow (≈6 minutes)

1. **Intro (30s)**
   - Introduce the FBLA Virtual Pet challenge and goals (financial literacy + STEM).
   - Mention AI personalization, analytics, and accessibility upgrades.

2. **Registration & Pet Creation (1 min)**
   - Sign up or log in.
   - Highlight animated sprite, theme toggle, and color-blind mode.
   - Show notifications appearing when stats dip.

3. **Care Loop & Mini-Games (2 min)**
   - Trigger quick actions from the bubble menu (feed/play/bathe/rest).
   - Launch a mini-game (Fetch) to demonstrate animated gameplay, adaptive difficulty, and reward overlay.
   - Visit the shop to spend coins and show sound effects.

4. **Analytics & Reporting (1.5 min)**
   - Navigate to `/analytics`.
  - Explain daily card, weekly trend chart, expense pie, AI tips.
   - Export a CSV and mention offline caching.

5. **Closing (1 min)**
   - Summarize financial learning outcomes and accessibility wins.
   - Mention upcoming roadmap (multi-pet, social features).
   - Invite questions.

## Pre-recorded Video Tips
- Capture at 1080p with voiceover following the flow above.
- Ensure the color-blind mode and notification overlay are demonstrated.
- Store the recording in a cloud drive (`docs/demo-video.mp4` ships with the repo as a placeholder; replace with your narrated clip).

## Technical Checklist
- ✅ Backend & frontend running locally
- ✅ Ambient music toggle tested (muted on stage start)
- ✅ CSV export verified (downloads folder empty beforehand)
- ✅ Browser cache cleared to show offline banner (optional)

## Judges’ FAQ
- **Q:** How is AI used?
  **A:** AI generates mood-based reactions, streak bonuses, and analytics suggestions.
- **Q:** How do students learn finance?
  **A:** Earning/spending coins, analytics insights, and expense tracking reinforce budgeting behaviour.
- **Q:** Accessibility?
  **A:** Theme/color-blind toggle, keyboard nav, ARIA labels, offline cache.
